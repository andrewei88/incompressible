Compression Rules

The Test
☐ Does removing it change what the reader does or believes? Keep.
☐ Does it only reduce entertainment or persuasion? Cut.
Survives
☐ Numbers ("incompressible by nature"), named beneficiaries, quotable lines.
☐ Satirical phrasing where the wording IS the argument.
Gets Cut
☐ Anecdotes (keep the point), repetition, social proof, SEO.
Faithfulness
☐ Every claim traces to the original. Autoresearch pass catches accuracy issues.
